Welcome

This project examines how Artificial Intelligence (AI) should be designed and deployed responsibly. It presents ethical principles, common risks, and practical steps developers and organisations can take to make AI systems fair, safe, and accountable.

What is AI Ethics?

AI ethics is a set of values and practices that guide how AI is built and used, ensuring technologies respect human rights, dignity, and social wellbeing.

Why it Matters

AI influences decisions about jobs, finance, health and justice. Poorly designed systems can cause bias, invade privacy, or reduce accountability.

Who is Responsible?

Developers, companies, governments and users all share responsibility for ethical AI through design choices, governance, and laws.
